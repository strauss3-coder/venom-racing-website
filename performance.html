Venom Performance tuning software from Stage 1 to Stage 3, with additional options such as AdBlue removal, DPF programming, EGR shut-off and deletes, hard-cut limiters, pops and bangs, burble effect, injector scaling, MAP and MAF sensor scaling, maximum RPM limit increase, stop/start disable, sport display activation, DTC deletes, speed-limit removal, swirl flaps, exhaust flaps, launch control activation and modification, cylinder-on-demand off, idle RPM adjustment, EVAP off, decat programming, map-switching solutions and many more to choose from.

Accredited Advanced Dastek Unichip Dealer

Dastek Unichip Installations

Available for most vehicles for optimisation of fuelling, boost control, throttle control and timing for maximum safe power tuning results. For vehicles where direct ECU flashing is locked by the manufacturer or undesirable due to warranty constraints, we install and calibrate Dastek Unichip piggyback microcontrollers — connecting directly to the sensor array to modify signals in real time, without altering the factory ECU file.
