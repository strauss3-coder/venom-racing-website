ECU Calibration & Remapping

Optimising air-fuel ratio, ignition timing and boost pressure is at the core of everything we do — every calibration is installed and validated in-house, then proven on our dyno before your vehicle leaves the workshop.

For most modern and utility vehicles, we achieve this through our official Dastek Unichip installations — see below for how the 5-map system works.

Official Dastek Unichip Installer

Dastek Unichip Installations

For vehicles where direct ECU flashing is locked by the manufacturer or undesirable due to warranty constraints, we install and calibrate Dastek Unichip piggyback microcontrollers — connecting directly to the sensor array to modify signals in real time, without altering the factory ECU file.

5-Map Switch Architecture

Map Function
1. ImmobiliserAnti-theft — disables injector signals
2. StockFactory baseline parameters
3. TowingHigh low-end torque within safe EGT limits
4. ConsumptionOptimised for fuel efficiency
5. PerformanceMaximum power under full throttle
