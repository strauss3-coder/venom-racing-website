Official Dastek Unichip Installer

Dastek Unichip Installations

For vehicles where direct ECU flashing is locked by the manufacturer or undesirable due to warranty constraints, we install and calibrate Dastek Unichip piggyback microcontrollers — connecting directly to the sensor array to modify signals in real time, without altering the factory ECU file.

5-Map Switch Architecture

Map Function
