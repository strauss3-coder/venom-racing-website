About Venom Racing

Precision Engineering, Proven On The Dyno

Venom Racing is a performance workshop based in eMalahleni, Mpumalanga, built around official brand partnerships and in-house fabrication rather than backyard guesswork.

As an official MRC Tuning and Dastek Unichip dealer, we write and install brand-authorised ECU and TCU calibrations, backed by our own dyno facility to validate every result before a vehicle leaves the workshop.
