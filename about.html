About Venom Racing

Precision Tuning
With Validated Results

Venom Racing is a performance workshop based in eMalahleni, Mpumalanga, built around official dealer accreditation, in-house fabrication and tune validation with data logging.

Venom Racing is equipped with the latest state-of-the-art diagnostic, data logging and ECU tuning equipment, allowing every vehicle to be tuned safely, accurately and reliably. Our investment in modern technology ensures every calibration is validated for maximum performance, drivability and long-term reliability.
